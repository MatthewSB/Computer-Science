Definitions

A motherboard is a printed circuit board containing the principal components of a computer or other device, with connections into which other circuit boards can be slotted.

The CPU also known as the Centeral Processing unit is the brain of your computer it handles all of your instructions given to your computer.

RAM is considered volatile memory wich means that the stored information is lost when there is no power RAM is used by the CPU.

A harddrive is a high-capacity, self-contained storage device containing a read-write mechanism plus one or more hard disks, inside a sealed unit.
