Describing Vindinium

What is Vindinium.org?

Vindinium.org is a place where people can battle other people with bots that they have coded. That way to win is in 1200 turns you need to capture as many mines as you can to collect gold. Every turn you will lose 1 health but if you get down to 1 health you will not die. The way to heal is by going to a tavern. Taverns are around the map and look like Root Beer Glasses. The taverns heal for 50 heath and cost 2 gold to buy a drink.s
